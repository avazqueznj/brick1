# ⭐ **2. WHAT YOU LOSE (exactly two things):**

You nailed it:

## ❌ **A. WiFi + WiFiClientSecure (TLS)**

This is the only big subsystem highly tied to Mbed in the Arduino GIGA ecosystem.

STM32duino **does NOT** include:

* WiFi drivers
* TLS sockets
* Secure TCP streams
* Certificate management
* DHCP/IP stack (unless you add LwIP separately)

Because Nucleos don’t have built-in WiFi.

### FIX

Use an **ESP32-C3 module** (AT mode or ESP-Hosted) to handle the entire TCP/TLS stack outside the STM32.

Brick will NOT need Mbed or WiFiSSLClient anymore.

## ❌ **B. QSPI Block Device + KVStore**

These will NOT exist in STM32duino.

**FATFS is still available.**
**QSPIFLASH is still available but you initialize it manually.**

Your "block access" layer must be rewritten with STM32 HAL QSPI driver.

This is MUCH simpler than rewriting WiFi/TLS.
